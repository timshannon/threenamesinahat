Three Names in a Hat

This simple party game is one my friends and family always enjoy playing. However the recent Covid-19 world-wide pandemic has made playing party games a little difficult. I felt it was the perfect quarantine project to turn this simple party game into one that could be played remotely while we all are social distancing.
