Rules

Setup

  1. Players are split between two teams
  2. Players take turns trying to get their team to guess the name they are given.
  3. Each round continues until there are no more names left.
  4. After 3 rounds the team with the most guessed names wins.

Rounds

  1. Give clues as long as it's not part of the person's name or a direct reference to their name. For example they can't do things like "rhymes with" or "starts with X" or "ends with X".
  2. Same as previous rounds, except no words, only acting out silent clues.
  3. Same as round one except this time you only get one word.
